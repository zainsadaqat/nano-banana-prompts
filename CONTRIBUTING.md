### Submission Process

1. **Fork** the repository
2. **Clone** your fork locally
3. **Create** a new branch: `git checkout -b add-[prompt-category]-prompts`
4. **Add** your prompts to the appropriate category in `README.md`
5. **Test** your prompts thoroughly
6. **Commit** with clear messages: `git commit -m "Add [number] new [category] prompts"`
7. **Push** to your fork: `git push origin add-[prompt-category]-prompts`
8. **Create** a Pull Request with:
   - Clear title describing what you're adding
   - List of prompts you're contributing
   - Brief explanation of why they're useful
